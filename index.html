💖 💖Welcome to Sam and Phil's Date Generator 💖 💖

We wanted a way for those couples to figure out a plan instead of just netflix and chilling. We wanted a lil spice in your life so we created a Date Generator with an weather display for your needs

  1. Type in where you are based in the city input to get the current weather of the day
  2. Select the type of activity you would like to partake in, in our drop down menu
  3. Select on the slider how expensive you would like the activity to be
  4. Click generate and go on your date

BONUS BONUS BONUS: Click Random if you don't have a preference for price or type
